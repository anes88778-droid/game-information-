GAME RULES

  • Guardian Dice: Use the dice to attack. A roll of 6 instantly resets a monster to the start!
  • Tree Defense: If a monster reaches the Sacred Tree, the Guardian loses the battle.
  • 10-Minute Trial: Survive the waves for 10 minutes to win and restore nature.

  • information: My idea is a dice game where in the centre a knight defends a tree and there are 4 monsters: one made of trash, another of sand, a normal one, and another ordinary monster. There are also cards. Every time a monster advances, it lands on a space according to luck. If the monsters meet on the same space, they return to the start. When the knight rolls the dice and, for example, gets a 3, and spins a pen that lands on one of the monsters, the player controlling that monster moves back 3 spaces depending on the knight's luck. This way, the knight can defend the tree. The cards work such that every time a monster moves backward, the knight's owner takes a card that describes an event that could happen in real life causing severe environmental pollution.
